About Us

The Food Network, established in August 2023 at Mountain House High School, has a clear and impactful mission. We are dedicated to addressing the pressing issue of food waste while simultaneously helping the homeless community. Our approach involves collaborating with local retailers to rescue surplus food items that would otherwise go to waste due to reasons like slow sales or minor imperfections. By doing so, we not only reduce food wastage but also minimize its environmental impact. Once we've collected this surplus food, our team meticulously sorts and prepares it, following strict hygiene standards to ensure safety. We then organize regular food distribution events at shelters and community centers, ensuring that rescued food reaches those who need it most. Our mission goes beyond providing nourishment. We aim to create a warm and welcoming community through our events, offering not only food but also a sense of belonging and support. As we continue to grow, we seek partnerships with local organizations, volunteers, and sponsors who share our vision of a world where no one goes hungry, and excess food finds a meaningful purpose. Together, we aspire to make a lasting difference in the lives of those in need while contributing to a more sustainable and compassionate society.
